You have here 3 dimensions of printable Frames and Sketchup sources. 
Re design it in OpenScad or blender could be great, I don't have the time.

All are compatible with all prusa i3 Drinted parts you can find, I drew it from Prusa's DXF. For big sized one, I decided to keep Y smooth rod distance
to keep upgrade "Standart -> double" cheap.
You can build a standard i3
You can build a enlarged i3, allowing you to you full print 20x20 with big extruders
You can go for the Double bed i3 (more will not be fin, 'cause of the bed weight). Design to handle big extruders (Yes I like direct infill) 

Design with brim and special wall for PLA to avoid blobs and proper surfaces



Upgrade to come : Enhancement of triangles, to mount Arduino and PSU cases without screws.
